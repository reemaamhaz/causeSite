About me

My Experience

I am a senior at New York University studying Computer Science and Data Science. I recently completed a software engineering internship under the Space Communications and Navigation (SCaN) Project at NASA. Previously, I interned as a Software Engineer at Allegiant Air. 


Aside from my professional experience, my independent research and work focuses on applications of data science in interstellar space travel, stellar orbits, and deep space networks.

My Skills

I have built large-scale and systems-based applications in C, Python, SQL, and Java. I've applied machine learning and data science to analyze data and predict trends using R and Python. Additionally, my front-end experience has equipped me with the skills to design responsive websites and deploy projects with AR/VR technology. Feel free to check the Projects page for more details!

My Goals

I am looking for an opportunity to work with a team that is working on cutting-edge technology to explore new frontiers. Ultimately, I would like to own a company that is at the forefront of innovation and curiosity.
